# Device Admin App - User Guide

## Welcome

Thank you for choosing our device financing program! This guide will help you understand how to use your financed device and the Device Admin App.

## What is the Device Admin App?

The Device Admin App helps you manage your device financing payments. It:
- Reminds you when payments are due
- Allows you to make payments directly from your phone
- Shows your payment history and schedule
- Protects your device and our investment

## Getting Started

### First Time Setup

When you receive your device, the store staff will help you:
1. Activate the app
2. Set up your payment method
3. Review your payment schedule
4. Answer any questions

### Understanding Your Payment Schedule

**Viewing Your Schedule:**
1. Open the Device Admin App
2. Tap "Payment Schedule" from the home screen
3. See all upcoming payments with dates and amounts

**Payment Status Colors:**
- **Blue:** Upcoming payment (not due yet)
- **Green:** Paid successfully
- **Red:** Overdue payment (needs immediate attention)

## Making Payments

### How to Pay

1. **Open the App**
   - Launch Device Admin App from your app drawer

2. **Go to Payments**
   - Tap "Make Payment" on home screen, OR
   - Tap "Payments" in bottom navigation

3. **Select Payment Amount**
   - Your due amount is shown automatically
   - You can pay more if you want to pay ahead

4. **Choose Payment Method**
   - Bank Transfer
   - Mobile Money (MTN, Airtel, etc.)
   - USSD Code
   - Debit/Credit Card

5. **Complete Payment**
   - Follow the instructions for your chosen method
   - Wait for confirmation (usually 1-2 minutes)
   - You'll see a success message when payment is confirmed

### Payment Confirmation

After successful payment:
- You'll receive a notification
- Payment will show as "Paid" in your schedule
- Your remaining balance will update
- Receipt will be saved in payment history

### Payment Methods Explained

**Bank Transfer:**
- Use your bank's mobile app
- Transfer to the account number shown
- Use your customer ID as reference

**Mobile Money:**
- Select your mobile money provider
- Follow the USSD prompts
- Confirm the transaction

**USSD Code:**
- Dial the code shown on screen
- Follow the menu options
- Enter your PIN to confirm

**Card Payment:**
- Enter your card details
- Verify with OTP
- Payment processes immediately

## Understanding Device Lock

### Why Does the Device Lock?

If a payment is missed, the device will lock after 24 hours to remind you to make your payment. This is temporary and will unlock as soon as you pay.

### What Happens When Locked?

**You CAN:**
- See the lock screen with payment information
- Make emergency calls (112, 911)
- View store contact information
- Make a payment to unlock

**You CANNOT:**
- Use other apps
- Access your files
- Make regular calls
- Use the internet (except for payment)

### How to Unlock Your Device

1. **On the Lock Screen:**
   - Tap "Pay Now" button
   - Complete your payment
   - Device unlocks automatically within 5 minutes

2. **If You Already Paid:**
   - Wait 5 minutes for automatic unlock
   - If still locked, tap "I Already Paid"
   - App will check with our system
   - Device unlocks if payment is confirmed

3. **Need Help?**
   - Call store using "Contact Store" button
   - Provide your customer ID
   - Store staff can verify payment and unlock remotely

### Avoiding Device Lock

- Pay on time (before due date)
- Set up automatic payments if available
- Keep internet connection active
- Respond to payment reminders promptly

## Troubleshooting

### Payment Not Showing as Paid

**If you paid but app still shows unpaid:**
1. Wait 5 minutes for sync
2. Check your internet connection
3. Tap "Force Sync" in Settings
4. If still not updated, contact store with:
   - Transaction reference number
   - Payment date and time
   - Amount paid

### App Not Opening

**If app crashes or won't open:**
1. Restart your device
2. Check for app updates
3. Ensure you have internet connection
4. Contact store if problem persists

### Notifications Not Appearing

**If you're not receiving reminders:**
1. Go to Android Settings
2. Apps → Device Admin App → Notifications
3. Ensure notifications are enabled
4. Check "Do Not Disturb" is not blocking

### Internet Connection Issues

**If app says "No Connection":**
1. Check your mobile data or Wi-Fi
2. Try switching between Wi-Fi and mobile data
3. Restart your device
4. App will queue actions and sync when online

### Device Locked But Payment Made

**If device is locked after payment:**
1. Wait 5 minutes for automatic unlock
2. Ensure internet connection is active
3. Tap "I Already Paid" on lock screen
4. Contact store if still locked after 15 minutes

## Important Information

### Payment Due Dates

- Payments are due on the date shown in your schedule
- Late payments may result in device lock
- Pay early to avoid any issues
- You can pay ahead if you want

### Grace Period

- If our system can't verify payment due to network issues
- You get 48 hours grace period
- Device won't lock during grace period
- Warning banner will show in app

### Data and Privacy

**What We Track:**
- Payment status and history

- App usage for support purposes
- Device status (locked/unlocked)

**We DO NOT Track:**
- Your personal messages or calls

### Security

**Protecting Your Device:**
- Don't try to uninstall the app
- Don't root or modify your device
- Don't share your customer ID
- Keep your payment method secure

**If Device is Lost or Stolen:**
1. Contact store immediately

3. We can track and lock the device
4. Police report may be required

## Completing Your Financing

### Final Payment

When you make your last payment:
- You'll receive a congratulations notification
- Payment schedule will show "Fully Paid"
- You'll be eligible for a release code

### Release Code

**What is it?**
- A special code that removes all restrictions
- Allows you to uninstall the app
- Gives you full control of your device

**How to Get It:**
1. Complete all payments
2. Contact store to request release code
3. Store verifies full payment
4. Release code sent via SMS

**How to Use It:**
1. Open Device Admin App
2. Go to Settings
3. Tap "Enter Release Code"
4. Enter the code you received
5. Tap "Submit"
6. Follow instructions to complete release

**After Release:**
- All restrictions removed
- You can uninstall the app
- Device is fully yours
- Factory reset is enabled

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can I pay more than the daily amount?**
A: Yes! You can pay ahead to reduce your balance faster.

**Q: What if I can't pay on time?**
A: Contact the store immediately. They may be able to help with payment arrangements.

**Q: Can I use the device without internet?**
A: Yes, but payment verification requires internet. Keep it connected when possible.

**Q: Will the app drain my battery?**
A: No, the app is optimized for minimal battery usage.

**Q: Can I remove the app?**
A: Not until you complete all payments and receive a release code.

**Q: What if I factory reset the device?**
A: Factory reset is disabled until full payment. Don't attempt it.

**Q: Can I sell the device?**
A: Not until you complete all payments and receive a release code.

**Q: What happens if I change my SIM card?**
A: The app continues to work normally. It's tied to the device, not the SIM.

**Q: How do I update the app?**
A: Updates happen automatically or through the store. Don't uninstall to update.

**Q: What if my device is damaged?**
A: Contact the store immediately. Payment obligations continue even if device is damaged.
